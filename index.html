Home

Our startup Wheels On Roll at first aims at incorporating the upcoming cycling culture of the campus and raising it to become a college lingo by making bike sharing a possibility while keeping the availability of cycles affordable and accessible at all times.

Encompassing new edge technology we plan at making the bike sharing manual free and app friendly under the project name "Digitally enabled automatic cycle rental with advance locking mechanism"

We also intend to spread to different campuses over a certain period of time. As a pilot run we have already launched 8 cycles which are running successfully on a stipulated Time for an year with 33% female customers

TARGET MARKET

We aim on spreading to different campuses{institutional and residential} and target the potential customers residing.

MOTIVATION

As a college student we have ourselves realised the need of cycles in our day to day life where in our focus is not on increasing the assets which comes with trouble of maintenances and disposing the cycles after the end of the tenure.

USP

Customers can relish the ownership of the cycles for the time period they have and our maintenance policies and services let them experience a hassle free renting tenure.
